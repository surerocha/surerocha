## 🎭 Arts & Research

For nearly 2 years, I was a dancer and performer in the *MiraIra* group (IFCE). I also developed research at the **Laboratory of Cultural and Traditional Practices (LPCT)** through a PIBIC-Jr grant, exploring cultural embodiment in popular traditions.

My research resulted in a published article:

> **COCOS DO CEARÁ: plural characteristics and playful embodiment**  
> SOUZA, Circe Macena de; BEZERRA, Sure Rocha.  
> In: *Proceedings of the 8th National Meeting of Dance Researchers (ANDA)*, 2024, Salvador.  
> Published by Galoá.  
> 🔗 [Read article](https://proceedings.science/anda-2024/trabalhos/cocos-do-ceara-caracteristicas-plurais-e-corporeidade-brincante?lang=pt-br)

I believe in integrating art, culture, and human perspectives into technology-based projects.
